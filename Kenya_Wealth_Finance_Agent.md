# 🇰🇪 Kenya Wealth & Finance Agent

The **Kenya Wealth & Finance Agent** is an AI-powered financial advisor specifically designed for the Kenyan economic landscape. It transforms complex financial data into personalized, actionable advice tailored to the unique needs of Kenyans.

## 🎯 What This Project Does

The agent acts as a comprehensive financial toolkit that helps users navigate the journey from basic budgeting to advanced wealth creation within the Kenyan context.

### 🛠 Key Capabilities
*   **Personalized Budgeting**: Adapts the 50/30/20 rule to the Kenyan lifestyle, integrating local habits like *Harambee* contributions and *Chama* savings.
*   **Strategic Savings**: Provides clear targets for emergency funds and recommends the best local vehicles for liquidity, from Money Market Funds (MMFs) to M-Shwari.
*   **Investment Guidance**: Offers a risk-tiered roadmap for investing in the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE), Treasury Bills (DhowCSD), SACCOs, and Real Estate.
*   **Tax Optimization**: Automates the calculation of PAYE, SHIF, NSSF, and the Housing Levy, helping users understand their true net take-home pay.
*   **Debt Management**: Provides critical comparisons between mobile loans (Fuliza, M-Shwari) and traditional bank or SACCO loans to help users avoid debt traps.
*   **Retirement Planning**: Guides users through mandatory NSSF contributions and voluntary individual pension schemes for long-term security.

## 🇰🇪 Why It's Different
Unlike general financial AI, this agent is "Kenya-Aware." It doesn't just talk about "savings accounts"; it talks about **SACCO dividends**, **T-Bills**, and **M-Akiba**. It understands the regulatory environment overseen by the CBK, CMA, and SASRA, ensuring that the advice is grounded in the local reality.
